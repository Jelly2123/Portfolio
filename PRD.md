# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Project Title

**Pragya Kashyap – Storytelling Portfolio Website**

Version: 1.0

Author: Pragya Kashyap

---

# 1. Vision

Build a premium, interactive portfolio website that tells my story through scroll-driven animations instead of traditional pages.

The website should create a "Wow" moment in the first few seconds and feel like a modern Apple or Stripe landing page.

Every scroll should reveal another chapter of my journey—from student to Laravel developer.

This portfolio is intended to impress recruiters, hiring managers, startups, and freelance clients.

---

# 2. Primary Goal

Visitors should:

* Learn who I am in under 30 seconds
* See my projects visually
* Understand my skills
* View my career journey
* Download my resume
* Visit my LinkedIn and GitHub
* Contact me easily
